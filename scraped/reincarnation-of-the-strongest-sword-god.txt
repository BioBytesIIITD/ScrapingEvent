After a night of drinking to cope with his sorrows, Shi Feng woke up to find himself ten years into the past… in a time when “God’s Domain” had first been released. With his memories and his ten years of experience, Shi Feng starts off on a journey back to the pinnacle. This time, he refuses to be controlled.
